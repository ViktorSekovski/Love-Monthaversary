Our Love Story

A journey through fate and fire

In this grand world,

with its infinite possibilities and countless souls...

We somehow

found each other

Our love burned bright

Overfed off its own momentum

Pretty soon, clear skies

got overwhelmed by clouds and darkness

and our love burned to ashes

Though filled with distractions,

obligations, even many suitors

The world tried to fill the void left behind

You could not focus on any of it

For none could compare to what was lost

The one that has your heart

was not around

And every moment felt incomplete without them

Your glance drifted away,

constantly on the lookout

for what was missing

Searching every horizon for a familiar silhouette

It took too long,

but I finally took off

on the right path

Courage finally overtook fear, hope conquered doubt

To win your heart once more

and unite the greatest love of our lives

To reclaim what should have never been lost

It did not start promisingly,

taking much persuasion

Words had to rebuild what silence had torn down

But gladly, love was on our side

A whisper from fate

was enough to take you away

And show you the truth your heart already knew

And show you how life can be together

What it should be like for both of us

Every moment a confirmation, every day a celebration

While now we must content ourselves

with letters and trinkets apart...

Each word a bridge across the distance between us

Soon we shall reunite

and all will be better than it has ever been

For this time, we know the value of what we have

Happy Monthaversary

Or seven monthaversary,

since we count both

Every single day a treasure, every moment a gift

I'm overjoyed

for having you!

Here's to us, to our journey, and to forever more
